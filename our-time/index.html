About

Not a family album.
A time archive.

Our Time is a long-term photographic project observing the same living room over six years.

The lens remains fixed at 35mm. The place and height stay similar. The camera is handheld. Because of that, each frame carries a slight human difference.

The room changes. The light changes. The view outside the window changes. Time passes through the same space and leaves a trace.

Project Structure

Lens 35mm fixed
Place Living room
Method Handheld, similar framing
Focus Space, light, time
Duration Six years
